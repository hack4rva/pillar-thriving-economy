# MVP Shapes - Thriving Economy

## Shape A - Procurement Opportunity Translator
### Best for
Teams with data parsing and UX strength.

### Inputs
- public procurement listings (portal, registry)
- plain-language glossary of terms

### Demo
Enter a business capability -> tool surfaces relevant solicitations and explains fit in plain language with links to official sources.

---

## Shape B - Small Business Setup Navigator
### Best for
Teams interested in service design, content design, and flow building.

### Inputs
- public guidance pages and documents
- a drafted decision tree (team-created via interviews or assumptions labeled)

### Demo
User answers a few questions -> tool outputs tailored steps, sequence, and links to official pages.

---

## Shape C - Trades Pathway Explorer
### Best for
Teams with research and storytelling skills.

### Inputs
- public info on programs, certifications, training providers
- curated content on timelines and prerequisites

### Demo
Interactive explainer that shows discover -> prepare -> persist path for a specific trade.

---

## Shape D - Staff/Nonprofit Workflow Helper
### Best for
Teams focused on intake summarization and referral support.

### Inputs
- intake template
- public service/resource directory

### Demo
Enter a short case summary -> tool suggests relevant info, resources, or next steps with links.

---

## Shape E - Opportunity & Resource Map/Dashboard
### Best for
Teams with data and frontend skills.

### Inputs
- one to three public datasets

### Demo
Map or dashboard with filters that connect business, workforce, or contracting indicators with explanatory context.
