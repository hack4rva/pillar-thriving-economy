# Demo Advice (90 seconds)

Structure
- Problem (10s): who, where, why it matters in Richmond.
- User (10s): one persona and their task.
- What we built (20s): show the working flow.
- Sources (20s): point to citations, show “View official page” handoff.
- Limits (10s): what it does not do (no eligibility/legal decisions).
- Next (20s): concrete continuation ask (owner, artifact, next dataset).

Tips
- Use real examples (2–5 items), not lorem ipsum.
- Keep claims narrow; avoid “platform” talk.
- Put disclaimers and source badges on screen.
