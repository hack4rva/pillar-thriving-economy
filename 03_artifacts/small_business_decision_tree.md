# Small Business Decision Tree

Purpose: Map order of operations and divergent rules by business type.

*Verified March 2026 against primary City/state/federal sources. Steps requiring human judgment are noted.*

---

## Top-Level Flow

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Choose business structure (LLC / Corp / Sole Prop / Partnership) and register with VA SCC | [VA SCC — CIS](https://www.scc.virginia.gov/pages/Business-Information) |
| 2 | Obtain IRS EIN if required (employees, partnership, corporation, excise taxes) | [IRS EIN guidance](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/employer-id-numbers) |
| 3 | Zoning check — obtain Certificate of Zoning Compliance (CZC) via Online Permit Portal | [City Zoning Administration](https://www.rva.gov/planning-and-development-review/zoning-administration) |
| 4 | Sector-specific approvals as needed (Health / ABC / DPOR contractor license) | See branches below |
| 5 | Register for state taxes with VA Dept of Taxation (sales tax, withholding, etc.) | [Virginia Tax — Register a Business](https://www.tax.virginia.gov/register-business) |
| 6 | Apply for BPOL license via RVA Business Portal | [E-007] https://rvapay.rva.gov/bpp |
| 7 | Check new-business incentive — $30 flat fee for first 2 years (qualifying businesses) | [E-008] https://www.rva.gov/finance/bpol-exemption (corrected 2026-03-18) |

> **Note:** City Zoning Administration must verify a valid CZC before BPOL renewal can be processed. Step 3 is effectively a prerequisite for Step 6.

## Branches by Business Type

### Home-Based Solo Service

- Obtain CZC with Home Occupation designation via Online Permit Portal (covers signage/parking/employee limits)
- Sole proprietors using a trade name: file DBA/assumed name certificate with VA SCC
- Typically no Health or ABC approvals required
- Proceed directly to BPOL [E-007]

### Food-Related / Regulated

- Submit plans and equipment specs to RCHD for plan review
- After plan review approval, submit foodservice permit application **at least 30 days before opening** [E-009]
- Certificate of Occupancy (CO) required before final health inspection can occur
- Allow 7–10 working days for opening inspection scheduling [E-009]
- If alcohol service: apply for VA ABC retail license — up to 60-day processing; 10 consecutive days public notice required [E-010]

### Brick-and-Mortar Retail or Office

- Certificate of Occupancy (CO) required; code compliance inspection
- Zoning CZC required before BPOL renewal

### Contractor

- Certificate of Occupancy (CO) required where applicable
- DPOR contractor license required by VA law — no person may engage in contracting work without a license (VA Code § 54.1-1100)
- Class A / B / C thresholds based on contract and 12-month project value
- Workers' compensation certificate required

## Points Requiring Human Guidance

These steps cannot be reliably automated and should direct users to staff or official contact:

- Zoning use interpretation for a specific address
- Variance / BZA pathways and timelines
- Complex assembly uses and shared kitchen arrangements
- iSupplier document checklist: confirmed = W-9 + ACH/EDI Payment Agreement + voided check/bank document (corrected 2026-03-18)
- BPOL calculation edge cases (contractors, peddlers, and certain exclusions)

---

## Key Sequencing Constraint

```
CZC (Step 3) → must precede → BPOL renewal (Step 6)
CO (sector-specific) → must precede → final health inspection (food businesses)
Plan review approval → must precede → foodservice permit application
```
