## Journey 1 — First-time minority-owned contractor exploring City work
- User goal: Understand if City has work matching capabilities and how to participate.
- Steps taken:
  - Searches City site; finds OpenGov portal for active solicitations [E-002]
  - Encounters iSupplier for vendor registration before payment eligibility [E-004]
  - Hears about OMBD MBE/ESB directory; explores partnership options [E-005]
  - Expands search to eVA for regional/state opportunities [E-003]
- Pages/systems touched: OpenGov, iSupplier, OMBD directory, eVA, Open Data (historical) [E-001]
- Friction points:
  - Fragmented platforms; multiple logins and monitoring [E-002][E-003]
  - iSupplier session timeout; document prep confusion [E-004]
  - Category code mapping (NAICS/NIGP) unclear
  - Interpreting bonding/insurance requirements from attachments
- User questions by step:
  - Do I need iSupplier before I bid or only for award/payment?
  - Which NAICS/NIGP codes match my services?
  - Am I eligible for MBE goals, and where do I prove it?
  - What does “mandatory pre-bid” mean and how do I attend?
- Prototype opportunities:
  - Unified “procurement feed” + code mapping helper
  - Pre-iSupplier checklist and timeout-safe upload guidance
  - Plain-language explanation of solicitation types and requirements

## Journey 2 — First-time home-based entrepreneur starting a business in Richmond
- User goal: Start a compliant home-based business with minimal delay.
- Steps taken:
  - Forms LLC with VA SCC; obtains IRS EIN (state/federal prerequisites)
  - Checks zoning and home-occupation rules in online permit portal (OPP)
  - Registers for BPOL via RVA Business Portal (digital-first) [E-007]
  - Reviews potential incentives (new-business BPOL adjustment) [E-008]
- Pages/systems touched: VA SCC, IRS EIN, Zoning/OPP, RVA Business Portal, Finance/BPOL
- Friction points:
  - Zoning pre-check ambiguity for certain uses (e.g., cottage food)
  - Sequencing: not clear which comes first (zoning vs. BPOL)
  - Digital literacy barriers for portal use (no paper option for 2025) [E-007]
- User questions by step:
  - Is my address eligible for my business type without a variance?
  - What documents do I need before starting BPOL?
  - Do I qualify for the new-business incentive?
- Prototype opportunities:
  - Decision-tree wizard with address/type-based guidance
  - Zoning pre-check tool tied to permitted uses
  - BPOL checklist with deadlines/penalty alerts
