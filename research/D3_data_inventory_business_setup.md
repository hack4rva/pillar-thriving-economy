# Source Inventory: Small-Business Navigation in Richmond

## Comprehensive Resource Guide for Starting a Business in Richmond, Virginia

The following source inventory consolidates all relevant materials for entrepreneurs and small-business owners navigating the Richmond business setup process. This inventory organizes municipal, state, and community resources by function and format, providing a complete reference for compliance, licensing, permitting, and operational requirements.

This source inventory provides a comprehensive reference for small-business navigation in Richmond. The materials span formation, licensing, permitting, compliance, and ongoing operational support across municipal, state, and federal agencies. Most resources are accessible online through web portals, with telephone support available through RVA 311 (804-646-7000 or dial 3-1-1 locally) during business hours Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
