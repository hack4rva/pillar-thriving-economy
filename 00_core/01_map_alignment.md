# MAP Alignment - Thriving Economy Pillar

## Context
Align projects to the City’s economic opportunity aims such as equitable participation in public contracting, small business growth, and clearer pathways into high-opportunity jobs.

## Example alignment areas
- Procurement equity: improve visibility and understanding of opportunities and processes for small and minority-owned businesses.
- Small business success: reduce friction in business setup and compliance through plain-language guidance and navigation.
- Workforce pathways: help residents discover, prepare for, and persist in trades and technical careers.

## Suggested indicators to reference (team-defined)
Note: Use publicly available indicators and clearly label assumptions.
- number/share of minority-owned firms engaging with procurement opportunities
- time-to-understand (or steps) for new business setup
- awareness/usage of workforce or entrepreneurship resources

## Implication for teams
High-value hackathon projects usually align to one of these:
- contracting access and navigation
- small business onboarding and compliance clarity
- workforce/training pathway visibility
- resource discovery and coordination
- city economic data exploration
