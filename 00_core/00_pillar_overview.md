# Pillar Overview - Thriving Economy

## Purpose
This repository is a markdown knowledge base for teams working on the Thriving Economy pillar in the Richmond Civic Hackathon. It helps teams understand the problem space, review problem statements, explore public data, and shape realistic weekend MVPs.

## Pillar framing
Thriving Economy focuses on helping Richmonders and local businesses participate in and benefit from economic opportunity, including:
- equitable access to City contracting opportunities
- small business formation and navigation
- workforce pathways into trades and technical careers
- entrepreneurship support and ecosystem visibility
- reducing friction in business setup and compliance

## Why this matters
Economic participation and small business success drive:
- income and wealth-building opportunities
- local job creation and retention
- more equitable access to public contracts
- stronger, more resilient neighborhoods

## Useful prototype directions
Prototypes that often help in this pillar:
- make public information easier to understand
- make resources easier to find and compare
- make public data easier to explore
- reveal actionable patterns for residents, businesses, and staff
- help frontline organizations triage or coordinate information

## Less useful directions
Prototypes that tend to struggle in a weekend:
- require policy changes or new legal authority
- require integration with official City systems
- make legal, licensing, or contracting determinations
- depend on private or unavailable data
